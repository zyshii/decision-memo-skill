# Quick Capture Template

Use this when the user wants to preserve material fast without committing to a full retrospective. The goal is to capture enough structure that the memo can be upgraded to Full Mode later without re-interviewing.

---

- **一句话概括**：[≤ 15 字]
- **状态**：⚡ Quick Capture（可后续升级为 Full Memo）
